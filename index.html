Our Company

Staff Alliance is a Filipino-owned corporation, duly registered with the Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC) with more than Php40 Million in paid-up capital, and registered with the Department of Labor and Employment (DOLE) under DO No. 174 and with the National Privacy Commission (NPC) under the Data Privacy Act of 2012.

Primarily an HR solutions provider, it caters to various industries that include banking, manufacturing, financing, insurance, real estate, hospitality, logistics, hospital, healthcare, food, and BPO, among others.

Living up to our core values of integrity, professionalism and honest service, we choose to take the road of hard work and perseverance to reach our goals. We strive to provide quality service at all times, by properly training our staff, constantly updating our systems and procedures, and diligently complying with labor laws and regulations.
