Our Company

Staff Alliance, Inc. is a wholly Filipino-owned corporation borne out of the ideal of genuine partnership. For us, being a true ally is nothing short of conducting our business in the highest standards of moral values. Since our initial operations in 1993, we have stayed true to this work ethic and thereby gained the trust and patronage of corporations.

As an HR solutions provider, Staff Alliance, Inc. continuously strives to make a difference in the industry where it belongs. We define our success through the lens of the identity we endeavor to create.

Outsourced Services Division

Serves companies who farm out business processes, providing competent and able-bodied staff for various business processes like coding, telemarketing, front desk, customer assistance, accounting, and technical processes.

Custodial Services Division

Addresses needs for long-term janitorial and messengerial services, providing efficient, reliable, and trustworthy staff.
